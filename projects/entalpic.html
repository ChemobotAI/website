Entalpic

Lab automation strategy and design support

We began working with Entalpic in 2024, before they even announced their company and funding round. Our initial engagement focused on helping the team understand how an automated lab is designed and the levels of automation possible – delivered through detailed reports and technical deliverables.

We now work with Entalpic on a weekly basis, providing ongoing consulting on their lab design and automation strategy as they scale their AI-driven chemistry platform.
